## Project Description

**Priority Training** is a web project we developed for a client in the firearms training and personal safety sector. This ***landing page*** is strategically designed to provide information about the services offered and facilitate contact with the instructor. It presents a professional image and aims to build trust with a target audience interested in self-defense training and the responsible use of firearms. This project demonstrates our ability to create informative and client-connection-oriented web solutions for specialized businesses.

* **Clear presentation of services:** Details of the courses offered (*NRA Basic Pistol Shooting*, *CCL/CCW Training*, *Eddie Eagle*, *Personal Pistol Training*).
* **Emphasis on the instructor's experience and qualifications:** Highlights NRA certification and experience in protection and security.
* **Testimonials section:** Incorporation of social proof to build credibility.
* **Professional and focused design:** Conveys seriousness and expertise in the sector.
* **Direct contact form:** Facilitates communication for inquiries and registrations.
* **Visible contact information:** Provides multiple ways for clients to get in touch.

## Technologies Used

- **WordPress:** CMS for content management.
- **Divi:** Page builder for visual design.

## Challenges and Learnings

* **Understanding the sensitivity of the sector:** Addressing the topic of firearms training required a careful approach to communication and design to convey professionalism, safety, and responsibility.
* **Presenting the value proposition clearly:** Highlighting the benefits of each course and the instructor's experience concisely and persuasively for a specific audience.
* **Building trust in a specialized service:** Utilizing elements such as testimonials and the detailed description of the instructor's qualifications to build credibility.
* **Designing intuitive navigation:** Ensuring that users can easily find relevant information about the services, the instructor, and how to contact them.
* **Optimization for lead capture:** Implementing an accessible and visible contact form to make it easy for potential clients to request more information or register for courses.

## Results

At Priority Training, our focus on an ***informative*** design and a *direct* user experience resulted in an **efficient** website for communicating training services. We clearly presented the course offerings and the instructor's experience, making it easy for visitors to understand the options and providing the means to establish contact simply. The inclusion of testimonials helped build trust in the quality of the training offered.
